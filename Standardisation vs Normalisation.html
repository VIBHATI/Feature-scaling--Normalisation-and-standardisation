Generating data sampled from normal, poisson and uniform distribution and visualizing the effect of applying MinMaxScaler and StandardScaler on data distribution. Normal or gaussian distributions are observed in the real world in several scneraios including height of the population, Income distribution in an economy. Poisson distribution is a skewed distribution and is typically observed in retail sales data. When we roll a die or toss an unbiased coin the observations follow a uniform distribution.
